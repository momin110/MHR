Job description

As a Junior Web Developer at Hot Beans, you will play a key role in supporting the development and maintenance of web applications. This entry-level position is ideal for a passionate and motivated individual looking to kickstart their career in web development

Job requirements

Responsibilities:

Coding and Development:

Assist in the development of web applications using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Collaborate with team members to implement new features and enhancements.

Testing and Debugging:

Participate in testing processes to identify and fix bugs or issues. Work closely with senior developers to ensure code quality and application stability.

Documentation:

Document code changes and updates for future reference. Contribute to the development of internal documentation related to projects.

Collaboration:

Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including designers and other developers. Participate in team meetings to understand project requirements and goals.

Learning and Development:

Actively engage in training programs to enhance coding skills and stay updated on industry trends. Seek guidance from senior team members to accelerate your learning curve.

Adherence to Best Practices:

Follow coding standards and best practices established within the company. Adapt to version control and project management processes.

Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience). Basic knowledge of web development technologies, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Familiarity with web development frameworks is a plus. Strong problem-solving and analytical skills. Excellent communication and teamwork skills. Eagerness to learn and adapt to new technologies.

Benefits:

Health insurance coverage Professional development opportunities Dynamic and collaborative work environment
